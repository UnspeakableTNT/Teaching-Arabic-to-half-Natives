Learn About ن (Noon)

Appearance of ن (Noon)

Beginning: نـ (e.g., نور - Noor)

Middle: ـنـ (e.g., فنان - Fannan)

End: ـن (e.g., بن - Ban)

Pronunciation of ن (Noon)

The letter ن (Noon) is pronounced as 'n' in 'noon'. It's a nasal consonant sound.
