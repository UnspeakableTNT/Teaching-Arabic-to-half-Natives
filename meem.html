Learn About م (Meem)

Appearance of م (Meem)

Beginning: مـ (e.g., مدرسة - Madrasa)

Middle: ـمـ (e.g., عمل - Amal)

End: ـم (e.g., نظام - Nizam)

Pronunciation of م (Meem)

The letter م (Meem) is pronounced as 'm' in 'moon'. It's a bilabial nasal consonant.
