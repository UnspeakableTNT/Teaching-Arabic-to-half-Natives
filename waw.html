Learn About و (Waw)

Appearance of و (Waw)

Beginning: وـ (e.g., ورد - Ward)

Middle (Alif Rule): ـو (e.g., ولد - Walad)

End: ـو (e.g., جملة - Jumlah)

Pronunciation of و (Waw)

The letter و (Waw) is pronounced as 'w' in 'water'. It's a voiced bilabial glide sound.
