Learn About ل (Lam)

Appearance of ل (Lam)

Beginning: لـ (e.g., لعبة - Toy)

Middle: ـلـ (e.g., بلد - Country)

End: ـل (e.g., جمل - Camel)

Pronunciation of ل (Lam)

The letter ل (Lam) is pronounced as 'l' in 'lion'. It's a voiced dental lateral approximant.
